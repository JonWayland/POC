Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At The Dollar Web, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services (collectively, the "Services").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using our Services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by the terms of this Privacy Policy.

The Dollar Web is built on the principle of privacy. Our tools operate entirely within your browser, and we do not receive, view, or store any documents you upload or content you create with our tools.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be clear about the data we do not collect:

  • We do not collect, access, or store any documents, files, or data that you upload to our Services.
  • We do not see the content of PDFs, QR codes, invoices, or any other outputs you create using our tools.
  • We do not know what specific information you input into our tools (such as text entered or images uploaded).
  • We do not track what you download from our Services.

2.1 Client-Side Processing

All of our tools process data locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your files and content never leave your device; they are stored only in your browser's localStorage until you clear your browsing data or cache.

3. Information We Do Collect

While we respect your privacy by not collecting document data, we do collect certain usage information to help us improve our Services:

3.1 Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage data through our analytics.js file, which helps us understand how our tools are being used. This includes:

  • Which tools you use on our website
  • When you interact with specific features (such as clicking "Download" or "Generate")
  • How frequently our tools are used
  • General user flow through our website
  • Session duration and pages visited

Importantly, we only track the fact that an action occurred (e.g., "PDF download button clicked"), but not what was processed or downloaded (e.g., we don't know the content of the PDF).

What We Track What We Don't Track
Tool usage (which tools are used) Content of uploaded files
Feature interactions (buttons clicked) Text entered in form fields
Time spent on specific tools Images or documents created
Browser and device information Downloaded file contents
Referral source (how you found us) Personal or sensitive information

3.2 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information about your device and browser, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Device type
  • Screen resolution
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Language preferences

4. How We Use Collected Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • To improve our Services based on how they are being used
  • To identify and fix technical issues
  • To analyze which features are most valuable to users
  • To understand user preferences and optimize user experience
  • To develop new features based on observed usage patterns

4.1 Analytics

We use Supabase to collect and analyze anonymous usage data. This helps us make data-driven decisions about which features to improve or develop.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

Our Services use localStorage, a feature of your browser, to store your preferences and any in-progress work. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

We may use cookies for analytics purposes to track anonymous usage data. You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, but this may limit your ability to use some features of our Services.

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. The anonymous usage data we collect is processed using Supabase, our analytics provider.

6.1 Third-Party Services

Our website may include links to third-party websites, plugins, and applications. Clicking on those links may allow third parties to collect or share data about you. We do not control these third-party websites and are not responsible for their privacy statements.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Since your documents and created content remain on your device, their security largely depends on your own device security. We recommend keeping your operating system and browser updated to ensure the best protection.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove that information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the bottom of this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, such as:

  • The right to access information we have about you
  • The right to request that we delete any data we have about you
  • The right to object to processing of your data
  • The right to data portability

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

11. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
